Crystals Collector!

Directions

At the start of each game, you will be given a random number (# to Match).

There are 4 crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

WIN by matching "Your Score" to the "# to Match".

LOSE if "Your Score" goes higher than "# to Match".

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

For every new game, the value of each crystal will change.
